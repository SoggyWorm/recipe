How to Make Pasta Like a Walking "W"

Estimated prep time: 1 hour
Serves: Around 7-8 people

I chose pasta because it was easiest to make. I usually just make pasta when I feel lazy. You don't need a recipe for it and it's very simple and easy. You could probably teach a newborn how to make this pasta dish. Even if you think you've messed it up, a wise man by the name of Master Oogway once said that "There are no accidents", unless, you know, you burn your house down...

Allergy Info

Pasta contains:

  • Gluten
  • Wheat

Else:

  • Milk (cheese)
  • Tomato pasta used in the recipe was naturally gluten free. Read your own labels before usage consumption.

Ingredients:

  • Penne Pasta (Preferably regularly sized)
  • Basil (dried)
  • Tomato Sauce OR Marinara Sauce
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese (Low moisture)
  • Thyme
  • Water
  • Olive Oil
  • A tomato (Optional)
  • Salt (Pepper is also optional)
  • Mild Cheddar Cheese

Boil Your Pasta

  • Bring some water to a boil
  • After your water is done boiling, pour the pasta in and boil until soft.

Add Some Spices and the Sauce

  • Add your herbs:
    • Basil
    • Thyme
    • Italian Seasoning
  • Add Parmesan Cheese and a very small amount of Olive Oil
  • Add the sauce
  • Stir the mixture until pasta is fully coated
  • You can also add in a bit of cheddar and mozzarella cheese

Sprinkle on Some Cheese

  • Pour your pasta into a pan. It's recommended that you bake you pasta in a glass pan.
  • Sprinkle on some Cheddar, Mozzarella, and some more basil or any other herbs

Bake it!

  • Bake in the oven at 375Â°F until the cheese is melted and browned

And then you're done!
